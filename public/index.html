How It Works

Effortless Pre-Booking

Users pre-book takeout orders for convenient on-the-go pick-up. BriskIT calculates your ETA and releases orders to restaurants, ensuring a seamless experience.

Dedicated BriskIT Runners

Ensuring food safety and quality, our runners collect orders from restaurants and deposit them into temperature-controlled pick-up machine slots.

Quick & Convenient Pickup

Touch-screen enabled machines with barcode scanning enable customers to complete the pick-up process in under 2 minutes.

Strategic Locations

Pick-up machines are strategically placed at vantage points for easy access, eliminating the need to enter restaurants or wait in lines.
